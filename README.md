# Thailand Drone Trip Planner

A simple, client-side planning tool from GlobeKit to help you organize drone travel requirements and generate a portable trip plan before you travel.

## What this tool does

- Organizes key drone travel requirements for Thailand  
- Helps you track registration, insurance, and airspace checks  
- Stores your progress locally in your browser  
- Generates a downloadable HTML travel plan for use during your trip  

## How to use

1. Open the planner  
2. Fill in each section as you research your trip  
3. Mark steps as complete as you go  
4. Generate your plan  
5. Download the plan and keep it accessible during travel  

## Important

This tool is a planning aid only.  
You are responsible for verifying all requirements with official sources before traveling or flying.
